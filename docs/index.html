Ten
Dots.

A quiet number puzzle for iPhone in the spirit of the pen-and-paper classic. Tap two numbers that match or sum to ten. Clear the board.

v1.0.0·iPhone · iOS 16+

01

Overview

How to play

You start with nineteen numbers laid out across the board. Tap two cells to clear them — they must be either the same value or sum to ten.

  • The two cells must be next to each other in reading order, in the same column, or on a diagonal — with only crossed-out cells between them.
  • Tap Hint to highlight one valid pair.
  • When you're stuck, tap Add to copy the remaining numbers to the bottom and keep going.
  • Goal: clear the whole board. Best score is the fewest adds.
02

Origin

Is this a clone of something?

No. The match-the-same-or-sum-to-ten mechanic is a pen-and-paper puzzle from before the App Store existed (you may have seen it as "Take Ten" or "Numerica"). TenDots is an original implementation with its own UI and code — no assets, naming, or branding from any specific app are reused.

03

Strategy

Tips that actually help

Two habits make a real difference: clear pairs that bridge the end of one row to the start of the next (those slots disappear faster than you'd think), and prefer matches that crush cells in the middle of a column before they get stuck behind other live numbers.

04

Data

What does the app remember?

Only your fewest-adds best, your total board-clears, and whether you've toggled haptics off. Everything else is the current board state, which lives only in memory until you reset.
