Features

What's in the build

The whole feature set, plainly listed — no upsells gating the basics.

◐

Two simple rules

Match same value OR sum to ten. Cells must be next to each other in reading order, same column, or diagonal — with only crossed-out cells between.

✦

Hint button

When you're stuck, Hint highlights one valid pair. When no pair exists, it tells you.

+

Add appends

Stuck? Tap Add to copy the remaining live numbers to the bottom of the board. Try again.

◷

Fewer adds wins

Your best score for the board is the fewest adds used to clear it. The lower the better.

◍

Classic opening

Starts with the standard 19-number seed used by the pen-and-paper original. Same opening every fresh game; the play diverges from there.

⊘

Local everything

Best score, total clears, haptics toggle — all on-device. Nothing transmitted.

How to play

From cold-start to good-at-it

A few short steps. The app gets out of your way after that.

01

Tap two numbers

Either same value (e.g. 4 + 4) or sum to ten (e.g. 3 + 7). The pair gets crossed out.

02

Mind adjacency

The two cells must be next to each other in reading order, the same column, or on a diagonal — with only crossed-out cells between them.

03

When stuck, hint or add

Hint highlights one valid pair. Add copies remaining numbers to the bottom of the board.

04

Clear the whole board

Goal: cross out every cell. The fewer Adds you used, the better the score.
