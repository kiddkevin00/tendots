Support

Frequently asked, honestly answered.

If your question isn't here, open an issue on GitHub. Public, traceable, and the same channel I use for everything else.

Is this a clone of any specific app?

No. The same-or-sum-to-ten mechanic is a pen-and-paper puzzle from before the App Store existed (sometimes called Take Ten or Numerica). TenDots is an original implementation with its own UI and code.

What counts as adjacent?

Two cells are adjacent if every cell strictly between them is already crossed out, in one of three orderings: reading order (left-to-right, top-to-bottom), same column, or same diagonal.

Can I undo a wrong match?

No undo by design — it would trivialize the puzzle. Restart if you want a fresh board.

What does the high score mean?

Lowest adds-to-clear you've achieved. The first time you clear, that becomes your best; subsequent clears with fewer adds replace it.

Is anything sent anywhere?

No. Best score, total clears, and the haptics setting are stored on this device only via AsyncStorage. No analytics, no third-party SDKs.
