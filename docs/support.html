Support

Frequently asked, honestly answered.

If your question isn't here, open an issue on GitHub. Public, traceable, and the same channel I use for everything else.

Is this a clone of something?

No. The same-or-sum-to-ten mechanic is a pen-and-paper puzzle that predates the App Store (Take Ten / Numerica). TenDots is an original implementation with its own UI, code, and branding.

Is there a daily puzzle?

Not yet. The opening sequence is fixed and deterministic; the variability comes from the order you choose to clear pairs.

Can I undo?

Not yet. Reset clears the board to start over. Per-move undo is on the roadmap.

What's the absolute minimum adds?

Depends on play order. Aim for zero adds — it's possible on the standard opening with very careful play.

Get in touch

Need help, or want to report a bug?

Open an issue on GitHub. There's no support email — issues are the canonical channel and they're public, which keeps everything honest.
